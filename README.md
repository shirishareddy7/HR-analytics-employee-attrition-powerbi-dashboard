# 📊 HR Analytics – Employee Attrition & Retention Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🧾 Project Overview
This project showcases an interactive HR Analytics dashboard built using Power BI to analyze employee attrition and retention trends. It focuses on identifying key workforce patterns and enabling real-time, data-driven HR decision-making through dynamic visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Purpose
To leverage Power BI for analyzing employee data and uncovering insights related to attrition, workforce distribution, and employee engagement factors.

---

## 📂 Dataset Used
- HR_1 & HR_2 datasets  
- Data Type: Structured HR Data (Excel Source)  
- Volume: 50K+ records per dataset  

---

## 📊 Business Questions (KPIs)
- What is the attrition rate across departments?  
- How does monthly income impact attrition?  
- What is the average working tenure across departments?  
- How does work-life balance vary by job role?  
- What is the relationship between attrition and years since last promotion?  
- What is the average hourly rate for male Research Scientists?  

---

## ⚙️ Power BI Implementation
- Performed data transformation using **Power Query Editor**  
- Built a **data model** by establishing relationships between tables  
- Created **DAX measures** for attrition rate, averages, and KPIs  
- Designed interactive visuals including bar charts, line charts, and KPI cards  
- Implemented **slicers (filters)** for department, gender, job role, and age group  
- Optimized dashboard for better user interaction and insights  

---

## 📌 PROJECT Insights
- Attrition rate remains consistently high (~50%), indicating retention challenges  
- Employees with **lower income levels show higher attrition trends**  
- Lack of promotions is strongly linked with higher employee turnover  
- Work-life balance varies across roles and impacts retention  
- Certain departments show slightly higher attrition compared to others  
- Younger employees and specific age groups show different attrition patterns  

---

## ✅ Final Conclusion
The Power BI dashboard effectively highlights key factors influencing employee attrition, including income, promotions, and work-life balance. 
It enables HR teams to monitor workforce trends interactively and take strategic actions to improve employee retention.
